Pizza is simple and delicious

It's good for the soul.

Hardware

  • Oven
  • Paddle or Spatula
  • Pizza wheel or long knife
  • Ladle or big spoon
  • Rolling pin

Software

  • Flour
  • Yeast
  • Water
  • Oil
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Marinara of preference
  • Mozzerella or provolone cheese

Alogorithm

  1. Mix the dough
  2. Let dough proof
  3. Portion Dough
  4. Roll dough out
  5. Let rest and relax
  6. Spread marinara evenly over dough
  7. Spread cheese evenly over marinara
  8. Bake
  9. Remove before burned
  10. Be full
