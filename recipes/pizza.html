Hardware

Alogorithm

  1. Mix the dough
  2. Let dough proof
  3. Portion Dough
  4. Roll dough out
  5. Let rest and relax
  6. Marinara even over dough
  7. Spread cheese evenly over marinara
  8. Bake
  9. Remove before burned
  10. Be full
